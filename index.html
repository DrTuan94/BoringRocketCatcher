Privacy Policy

Boring Rocket Catcher

Effective date: July 13, 2026

Overview

Boring Rocket Catcher respects your privacy. The app does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Data Collection

Boring Rocket Catcher does not collect personal information from users. The app does not use advertising trackers, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking services.

Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores gameplay information locally on your device, such as high scores, sound settings, music settings, haptic settings, unlocked achievements, unlocked skins, daily challenge progress, and tutorial status.

This information is used only for app functionality and is not sent to our servers.

Game Center

Boring Rocket Catcher may use Apple Game Center for leaderboards and score submission. Game Center is provided by Apple, and your use of Game Center is governed by Apple's privacy policy and your Apple account settings.

Sharing

The app may let you share score or unlock screenshots using the system share sheet. Sharing only happens when you choose to use that feature.

Children's Privacy

Boring Rocket Catcher does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
